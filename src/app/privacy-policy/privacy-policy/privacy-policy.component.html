Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Indaccu Services Pvt. Ltd. and its affiliates (collectively "Indaccu Solutions Books", we, our, us) collect, use, share, protect or otherwise process your information / personal data through our online books platform (hereinafter referred to as Platform).

Our Platform offers physical books, eBooks, audiobooks, and other reading and educational materials. By visiting this Platform, placing an order, or downloading any content, you expressly agree to be bound by the terms of this Privacy Policy and consent to our data practices described herein.

01

Information We Collect

We collect personal data when you register, browse, purchase, or interact with our Platform. The information we may collect includes but is not limited to:

  • Account details such as your name, email address, mobile number, and date of birth provided during registration
  • Delivery address and billing address for physical book orders and courier services
  • Payment information processed securely through our trusted payment gateway partners
  • Reading preferences, wish-listed titles, browsing history, and genre interests to personalise your experience
  • Device information, IP address, and browsing behaviour on our Platform
  • Communications you send us including order queries, reviews, and feedback
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal data collected to provide and improve our book selling services. Specifically, your data helps us to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders for physical books, eBooks, and audiobooks
  • Deliver purchased digital content (eBooks and audiobooks) securely to your account or registered device
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates, and delivery notifications
  • Personalise book recommendations based on your reading history, purchases, and genre preferences
  • Notify you about new arrivals, bestseller lists, author events, and promotional offers
  • Resolve disputes, handle returns, and troubleshoot platform issues
  • Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorised access, and misuse of digital content
03

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Logistics & Delivery Partners: We share your name and delivery address with courier and shipping partners solely to fulfil your physical book orders
  • Payment Processors: Payment details are shared with secure, PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways to process transactions
  • Publishers & Distributors: Limited order data may be shared with book publishers or authorised distributors to process special-order or pre-order titles
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government authority
  • Fraud Prevention: Information may be shared with relevant parties to investigate or prevent fraudulent orders or digital content piracy
04

Digital Content & Download Protection

For eBooks and audiobooks purchased on our Platform, we implement digital rights management (DRM) measures to protect intellectual property. Your download activity, device details, and access logs may be recorded to:

  • Prevent unauthorised sharing, copying, or redistribution of licensed digital content
  • Allow access to purchased eBooks across your registered devices as per our usage policy
  • Restore access to previously purchased digital titles if your device is lost or replaced

We do not share your reading activity or eBook download history with publishers without your explicit consent, except where required by licensing agreements or legal obligation.

05

Security Precautions

We adopt industry-standard security practices to protect your personal data from unauthorised access, disclosure, loss, or misuse. This includes:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our Platform
  • Secure, encrypted storage for sensitive account and payment information
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments of our systems

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and for any activity that occurs under your account.

06

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Specifically:

  • Order history and purchase records are retained for a minimum of 3 years for accounting and legal compliance purposes
  • eBook and audiobook licences remain associated with your account for the duration of the licence period
  • You may request deletion of your account at any time through your profile settings; upon deletion, your personal data will be removed except where retention is required by law
07

Your Rights

As a user of our Platform, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification: You may update or correct inaccurate information through your account settings
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt-Out: You may unsubscribe from marketing emails and book recommendation notifications at any time
  • Portability: You may request your purchase and account data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the details provided below.

08

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience on our Platform. These help us remember your preferences, track your reading wishlist, and analyse how our Platform is used. You may control cookie settings through your browser preferences, though disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Platform.

09

Consent

By visiting our Platform, creating an account, or purchasing any book or digital content, you consent to the collection, use, storage, and processing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. You may withdraw your consent at any time by writing to us, though this may limit your ability to use certain features of our Platform.

10

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our services, business practices, or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
