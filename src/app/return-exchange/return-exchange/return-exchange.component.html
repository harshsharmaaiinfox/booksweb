Return & Exchange

Shop with confidence — easy returns and exchanges

Last updated: March 2025

At Mangalbookstore, your satisfaction is our priority. If you are not completely happy with your purchase, we offer a straightforward return and exchange process.

Return Window

You may request a return within 7 days of receiving your order. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.

How to Initiate a Return

  • Log in to your account and go to "My Orders"
  • Select the order and item you wish to return
  • Choose a reason for return and submit your request
  • Our team will review and process your request within 2 business days

Exchange Policy

Exchanges are subject to product availability. If the requested size or variant is unavailable, a full refund will be issued. Exchange requests follow the same 7-day window as returns.

Non-Returnable Items

Items that have been worn, washed, altered, or damaged by the customer are not eligible for return or exchange. Innerwear, swimwear, and accessories marked as non-returnable at the time of purchase cannot be returned.
