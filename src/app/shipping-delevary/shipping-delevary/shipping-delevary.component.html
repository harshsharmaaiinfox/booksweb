Shipping & Delivery

Fast and reliable delivery to your doorstep

Last updated: March 2025

At Kahua Fashion Club, we are committed to delivering your orders quickly and reliably. Please review our shipping and delivery policy below.

Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. During sales or high-demand periods, processing may take an additional 1–2 days.

Delivery Time

Standard delivery takes 5–7 business days from the date of dispatch. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and availability of the product. Remote areas may take an additional 2–3 days.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on the order value, weight, and delivery location. We may offer free shipping on orders above a certain value — check the website for current offers.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can track your order status through your account dashboard or the courier partner's website.

Delivery Issues

If your order is delayed, damaged during transit, or not delivered, please contact our support team within 48 hours of the expected delivery date. We will investigate and resolve the issue promptly.
