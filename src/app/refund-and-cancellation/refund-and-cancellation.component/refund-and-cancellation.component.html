Refund & Return

Our commitment to your complete satisfaction

Last updated: March 2025

At Mangalbookstore, we want every shopping experience to be smooth and hassle-free. Below is our complete refund and cancellation policy to help you understand your rights and options.

01

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your order before it is shipped. Once an order has been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled — you will need to follow the return process after receiving the item.

To cancel an order, visit your account dashboard, go to "My Orders", and select the cancel option. Cancellations are processed immediately if the order has not yet been shipped.

02

Refund Policy

Refunds are initiated after the returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse. The inspection process typically takes 2–3 business days.

  • Online payments: Refund will be credited to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after approval.
  • Cash on Delivery: Refund will be processed via bank transfer. You will be asked to provide your bank details.
03

Partial Refunds

In certain cases, partial refunds may be issued if the returned item shows signs of use, is missing tags, or is not in its original condition. The refund amount will be determined after inspection.

04

Non-Refundable Scenarios

The following situations are not eligible for refunds or returns:

  • Items returned after the 7-day return window
  • Products marked as non-returnable at the time of purchase
  • Items damaged by the customer after delivery
  • Free gifts or promotional items
